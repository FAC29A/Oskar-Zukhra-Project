This page can be used to search historical records of earthquakes in your area, or any other area. Just enter the city, time period and search radius from your chosen location and records found will be displayed. Use table below as a reference to better understand how strong the recorded earthquakes were.

Richter Scale

Magnitude Category Effects
<1.0 to 2.9 micro generally not felt by people, though recorded on local instruments
3.0 - 3.9 minor felt by many people; no damage
4.0 - 4.9 light felt by all; minor breakage of objects
5.0 - 5.9 moderate some damage to weak structures
6.0 - 6.9 strong moderate damage in populated areas
7.0 - 7.9 major serious damage over large areas; loss of life
>=8.0 great severe destruction and loss of life over large areas
